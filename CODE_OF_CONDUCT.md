# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, professional, and inclusive environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the OWASP Web3 Wallet Security (WWS) project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience, identity, or status.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Accepting constructive feedback professionally  
- Focusing on improving the project and its security impact  
- Supporting collaboration and knowledge sharing  

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others' private information without permission  
- Disruptive, unprofessional, or harmful conduct  

---

## Security Responsibility

This project operates in a security-sensitive domain. All contributors are expected to:

- Follow responsible disclosure practices  
- Avoid sharing harmful exploit code without proper context  
- Use the materials for ethical security research and approved testing only  
- Not promote or engage in malicious or illegal activities  

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing contributions and discussions fairly  
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations  

Maintainers reserve the right to remove or modify contributions, and to restrict participation for individuals who violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories  
- Discussions and communication channels  
- Events or activities representing the project  

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers through official communication channels.

All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately. Confidentiality will be respected where possible.

---

## Disclaimer

This project is intended for educational, research, and professional security purposes only.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their actions comply with applicable laws and ethical standards. The project does not support or condone malicious activity.
